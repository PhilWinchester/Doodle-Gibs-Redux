DOODLE GIBS BATTLE

Think Plants vs Zombies meets your notebook.

Erasermen will spawn on the left side of the screen and you will be in charge of monitoring your resources and defending your base. Should an Eraserman reach the last line in your defensive grid they will deal damage to your castle and die. Careful because this can only happen a few times before you lose.

The Straw unit will shoot spitballs and chip away at the health of an Eraserman. The Match will blow up on contact with an Eraserman and both units will be removed.

Be careful which unit you use when! Good Luck xD!!
